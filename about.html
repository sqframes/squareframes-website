About Square Frames

Reliable hardware, timber, tools, and OHS supply — proudly supporting homes, trades, and businesses across Fiji.

Square Frames is a Suva-based supplier supporting homeowners, contractors, and businesses with hardware, tools, timber, OHS safety equipment, cleaning supplies, and dependable repair & maintenance products.
