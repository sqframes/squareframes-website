Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about quotes, delivery, timber sizes, standards, and safety supplies across Fiji.

Do you service areas outside Suva?
Yes. We service Suva, Nasinu, Nausori, Lami, Lautoka, Nadi, Labasa and all of Fiji. Delivery/logistics depend on location and item type.
How do I request a quote?
Click Request Quote in the header to open the enquiry form. Include item details, sizes, quantities (if known), and your location in Fiji for faster service.
What timber sizes do you supply?
Common sizes include 2x2, 3x2, 4x2, 6x2, 8x2, 10x2 and 12x2. Actual mill dimensions can vary by standard and supplier. Tell us the size + use-case and we’ll confirm availability and options.
Do you supply treated timber (H3 / H4)?
Yes. Treatment depends on application (above-ground exterior use, high exposure, ground contact, etc). Include your required treatment level (e.g., H3 or H4) in your enquiry and we’ll confirm availability.
Do your products meet building standards or specifications?
We can advise on suitable grades/treatments for common building use. If you require a specific standard, grade, treatment level, or certification, include it in your enquiry and we’ll confirm availability.
What plywood thicknesses do you stock?
Options vary by line. Common thicknesses include 4mm, 6mm, 9mm, 12mm, 16mm, 19mm and more. Share your required sheet size and thickness and we’ll confirm.
Do you supply OHS and safety equipment?
Yes. We supply a range of OHS and safety equipment including PPE and workplace safety products (hi-vis workwear, harnesses, helmets, gloves, traffic cones and signage).
Do you supply trade and business customers?
Yes. We support trade and business customers with responsive service and consistent supply. Send your list for a quote.
Do you deliver across Fiji?
We service customers across Fiji. Delivery/logistics depend on location and item type—send your location and list of items and we’ll advise the best option.
How can I speed up my quote response?
Include item names, sizes/dimensions, quantities (if known), delivery location, and any required standards or treatment levels. Add photos or a list if helpful.
