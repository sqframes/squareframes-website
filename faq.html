Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about timber sizes, standards, OHS safety supplies, tools, delivery areas, and how to request a quote in Fiji.

Timber Sizes Standards OHS / PPE Quotes
Do you service areas outside Suva?

Yes. Square Frames is based in Suva and services Nasinu, Nausori, Lami, Lautoka, Nadi, Labasa and all of Fiji.

How do I request a quote?

Click the Request Quote button on the header to open the enquiry form. Include the item name, quantities (if known), and your location in Fiji for faster service.

What timber sizes do you stock?

We stock common construction sizes such as 2x2 (50x50mm), 3x2 (75x50mm), 4x2 (100x50mm), 6x2 (150x50mm), 8x2 (200x50mm), 10x2 (250x50mm), and other sizes depending on availability.

Do you supply treated timber (e.g., H3)?

Yes. We can supply treated timber (such as H3 for above-ground protection). If you need a specific treatment level or use-case, include it in your enquiry so we can confirm availability.

Do you supply structural framing timber and dressed (planed) timber?

Yes. We supply structural framing timber (rough) as well as dressed/planed timber. Tell us the sizes and intended use and we will advise suitable options.

Do you supply plywood and sheet materials?

Yes. We supply plywood and sheet materials in commonly requested thicknesses and sizes. Send us the thickness and size you need and we’ll confirm availability and pricing.

Do you supply OHS / safety equipment and PPE?

Yes. We supply OHS and safety equipment including PPE such as gloves, helmets, harnesses, high-visibility wear, and other workplace safety items.

Can you help with product standards and compliance requirements?

Yes. If your project requires a particular standard, specification, grade or compliance requirement, tell us what you need and we will confirm suitable product options and availability.

Do you supply tools and consumables (screws, fixings, etc.)?

Yes. We supply hand tools, power tools, garden tools and consumables such as screws and fixings. If you can, include the size/spec you need in your request.

Why do some items show no price online?

Some prices vary based on size, specification, quantity or availability. Use the Request Quote form and we’ll confirm pricing and lead time.
