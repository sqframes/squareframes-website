General

Do you service areas outside Suva?
Yes. We service Suva, Nasinu, Nausori, Lami, Lautoka, Nadi, Labasa and all of Fiji.
Do you provide delivery across Fiji?
We service customers across Fiji. Delivery/logistics depend on location and item type—share your location and list of items and we’ll advise the best option.
How do I request a quote?
Use the Request Quote button in the header, or send an enquiry with item details, quantities (if known), and your location. We’ll reply with availability and pricing.
Do you supply trade and business customers?
Yes. We support trade and business customers with responsive service and consistent supply. Send your list for a quote.

Timber sizes

What timber sizes do you stock for framing?
Common framing sizes include 2x2, 3x2, 4x2, 6x2, 8x2, 10x2 and 12x2 (metric equivalents vary by finish and supplier). Stock depends on grade/treatment—send your size list and we’ll confirm availability.
Do you supply dressed/planed timber as well as rough timber?
Yes. We can supply dressed/planed timber as well as structural/rough options depending on your use. If you need a specific finish or tolerance, include it in your enquiry.
Do you supply treated timber (H3/H4)?
Yes. We can supply treated timber options depending on application (above-ground, ground contact, exposed use). Tell us how it will be used and we’ll recommend a suitable treatment level.

Standards & compliance

Do your timber and building products meet standard requirements?
We can advise on suitable grades/treatments for common building use. If you need a specific standard, grade, treatment level, or certification, include it in your enquiry and we’ll confirm availability.
Can you provide documentation or certifications for products?
Where available, we can provide product details or documentation from the supplier/manufacturer. Tell us exactly what you need (for example: timber treatment level, structural grade, compliance requirements) and we’ll confirm.
Do you supply products suitable for construction and site use?
Yes. We supply common construction materials and site-use items including timber, plywood, fasteners, tools and OHS/PPE. If your project has specific requirements, send the spec and we’ll match what’s available.

OHS / PPE

Do you supply OHS and safety equipment?
Yes. We supply OHS and safety equipment including PPE, high-visibility workwear, safety harnesses, gloves, helmets and workplace safety products.
Can you help choose the right PPE for a worksite?
Yes. If you tell us the job type and site conditions, we can suggest suitable PPE options and alternatives based on availability.

Send a quick enquiry and we’ll respond with the best option.
