Frequently Asked Questions

Products & Supply

What does Square Frames supply?

We supply hardware items, professional tools, timber products and building materials, OHS / safety equipment, cleaning supplies, and related consumables.

Do you supply OHS / safety equipment?

Yes — we supply a range of OHS safety equipment suitable for construction, industrial, and commercial use.

Can I request specific brands or product types?

Yes. Send your requirements (brand, specs, quantities) and we’ll advise availability and alternatives if needed.

Quotes & Ordering

How do I request a quote?

Use the enquiry form on our website, call us, or email us. For faster service, include your item list, quantities (if known), and your location.

What information should I include in an enquiry?

  • Item name / description (and specs or brand if known)
  • Quantity or estimated quantity
  • Your location (Suva / outside Suva)
  • Any deadline or delivery preference

Do you support trade and business customers?

Yes. We support businesses and contractors with dependable supply and responsive service.

Service Areas

Do you service areas outside Suva?

Yes. We are based in Suva and service customers throughout Fiji. If you’re outside Suva, contact us and we’ll advise the best way to support your request.

Which areas do you cover?

We service all of Fiji. Common areas include Suva, Nausori, Nadi, Lautoka, and Labasa, plus surrounding regions.

Support

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond quickly. Response time can vary based on demand and the detail in your enquiry.
