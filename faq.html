Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about timber sizes, standards, OHS safety equipment, tools and supply across Fiji.

Timber Sizes Standards OHS / PPE Nationwide Supply
Do you service areas outside Suva?

Yes. We service Suva, Nasinu, Nausori, Lami, Lautoka, Nadi, Labasa and all of Fiji.

What timber sizes do you stock?

Common sizes include 2x2, 3x2, 4x2, 6x2, 8x2 and 10x2. Availability may vary by grade and treatment.

Do you supply treated timber (H3)?

Yes. Treated timber suitable for above-ground construction is available. Let us know your requirements.

Do you supply OHS and safety equipment?

Yes. We supply PPE, safety harnesses, helmets, gloves, high-visibility clothing and safety accessories.

How do I request a quote?

Use the Request Quote button at the top of the page. Include item name, quantities and location.
